**Statement.** For every graph G with adjacency spectrum λ₁ ≥ … ≥ λₙ:

- **WoW 20:** n⁺(G) ≤ Σ_{λᵢ>0} λᵢ (number of positive eigenvalues ≤ half the graph energy)
